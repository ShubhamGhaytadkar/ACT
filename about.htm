What is ACTS?

Sanjivani ACTS inter-department student-run club, ACTS, as the name suggests, is the battlefield where students of Computer Technology students compete in sports, cultural, and management events. the club is an all-around experience and caters to every individual’s interest and skill set. We truly believe that students take home more than just memories, they take home skills, friendships, a sports person’s spirit, and the ACTS experience that is far more valuable than trophies and medals. Through the club, we become part of this big family that truly aspires to help one another grow.
